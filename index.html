Our Guiding Light

The Strong Humans Foundation is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to empowering veterans, first responders, and their families. Our mission is to foster healthy living and build strong relationships in the aftermath of traumatic brain injuries (TBI) and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD). We achieve this through natural and holistic treatment methods and activities, ensuring a comprehensive approach to healing and well-being.

Our Mission

We synchronize and enhance already established 501(c)(3) organizations through retreats utilizing different modalities of treatment through holistic and natural means. Our approach combines therapeutic activities - from equine therapy and mountain hiking to scuba diving and hunting expeditions - with education on nutrition and healthy living. These retreats include holistic dietitians, counselors, and subject matter experts in each therapeutic modality.

Healing Through Activity Therapy

Our comprehensive activity therapy programs connect veterans and first responders with diverse healing experiences. From hiking mountain trails and hunting expeditions to equine therapy and working with therapy animals, each activity is carefully designed to promote healing and personal growth. We also offer unique water-based therapies including scuba diving, surfing, and fishing - all guided by experienced professionals who understand the journey to recovery.

Diverse Therapeutic Activities

Our innovative therapy programs span land and sea, offering transformative experiences tailored to each participant's journey. Whether it's the meditative rhythm of ocean waves during surf therapy, the profound connection formed through equine partnerships, or the accomplishment felt after a challenging mountain hike, each activity serves as a pathway to healing. We believe in meeting veterans and first responders where they are, providing the therapeutic modality that resonates most deeply with their personal healing process.

Common Inquiries

What does your foundation do?

We provide holistic wellness programs and retreats for veterans, first responders, and their families dealing with TBI and PTSD.

Who is eligible for grants?

Veterans, first responders, and their immediate family members who are dealing with the effects of TBI or PTSD.

How are grants reviewed?

Applications are reviewed by our board based on need, impact potential, and alignment with our mission.

What types of activities do you offer?

Our activity therapy programs are diverse and tailored to individual needs. We offer hiking, hunting, fishing, scuba diving, surfing, equine therapy, animal therapy, and more. Each retreat features different activities based on participant needs and seasonal availability.

What is your nutrition program?

Our comprehensive nutrition education covers sourcing farm-fresh foods and meal preparation. Participants learn from holistic dietitians about selecting quality ingredients, building relationships with local farmers, and preparing nutritious meals that support healing from TBI and PTSD.
